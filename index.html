How to play

  1. Create a username - be inventive
  2. Work your way through the games in whichever order you wish to test your knowledge on each character.
  3. Once you have finished each game you will be presented with your score which will also record at the bottom of the page.
  4. Re-play the games as many times as you wish to try and beat your score.
